This is an extremely healthy drink I have almost every morning and great on detoxes as you get all your needed nutrients. You just have to play around to find the perfect drink. I find if you add beetroot, it makes it a fantastic color and gives extra flavor. To get the full benefit of the flavor, swish the juice around in your mouth; this is where lots of nutrients are digested.

Ingredients

  • 4 carrots, trimmed
  • 2 apples, quartered
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger

Steps

  1. Run carrots, apples, and celery through a juicer (alternating carrot, apple, and celery) according to manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Add ginger to juicer and process.
  3. Enjoy!
