Learn how to make white rice with perfect flavor. It's simple and easy, yet tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 cups white rice
  • Â¼ teaspoon garlic powder (Optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups boiling water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add rice, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Pour in boiling water to cover rice by about 1 inch; bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until water is absorbed and rice is tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
